# Sample Queries for Testing

## Product Search Queries

### Budget-Focused
- "Show me laptops under $600 for basic tasks"
- "Affordable wireless earbuds with good battery life"
- "Gaming keyboard under $80"

### Feature-Specific
- "Laptop with 16GB RAM and SSD for programming"
- "Smartphone with best camera for photography"
- "Noise cancelling headphones for travel"

### Use Case
- "Best laptop for video editing and graphic design"
- "Gaming laptop for competitive FPS games"
- "Tablet for reading and note-taking"

## Review Analysis Queries

### Sentiment
- "What do customers like about this laptop?"
- "Common complaints about gaming laptops"
- "Is the battery life good according to reviews?"

### Specific Features
- "What do reviews say about keyboard quality?"
- "Customer feedback on noise cancellation"
- "How's the customer service according to reviews?"

### Reliability
- "Is this brand reliable?"
- "Do these headphones break easily?"
- "Long-term durability based on reviews"

## Policy Questions

### Returns
- "What is your return policy?"
- "Can I return opened electronics?"
- "How long do I have to return a laptop?"

### Shipping
- "How fast is shipping?"
- "Do you offer free shipping?"
- "Can I track my order?"

### Warranty
- "What warranty do laptops come with?"
- "Is there extended warranty available?"
- "What does the warranty cover?"

### Payment
- "What payment methods do you accept?"
- "Do you offer payment plans?"
- "Can I use gift cards?"

## Comparison Queries

### Product Comparison
- "Compare MacBook Air vs Dell XPS 13"
- "iPhone 14 vs Samsung Galaxy S23"
- "Sony WH-1000XM5 vs Bose QuietComfort"

### Feature Comparison
- "Which laptop has better battery life: X or Y?"
- "Compare camera quality of iPhone vs Samsung"

## Complex Queries

### Multi-Constraint
- "Laptop with good battery life, under $1000, and 16GB RAM"
- "Wireless headphones under $150 with good reviews and noise cancellation"

### Vague Queries
- "Good laptop"
- "Help me choose headphones"
- "What should I buy for gaming?"

## Edge Cases

### Ambiguous
- "Is it worth it?" (without specifying product)
- "Tell me about X" (generic request)

### Out of Scope
- "How do I fix my laptop?" (technical support)
- "Where is my order?" (customer service)
